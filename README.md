# The Culprit

A party game for 4 to 10 players. A crime happened. Someone in the group did it. Everyone else has to figure out who.


## How to Play

One or more players are secretly assigned as Culprits. Everyone else is Innocent. Culprits know who they are. Innocents do not.

Each round goes like this:

1. A crime is revealed to the group.
2. Everyone introduces their job role. Culprits can lie about theirs.
3. Players discuss and share suspicions.
4. A clue is revealed.
5. Everyone votes on who they think is the Culprit. The player with the most votes is eliminated.

This repeats until someone wins.


## Winning

- Innocents win when all Culprits are eliminated.
- Culprits win when they equal or outnumber the Innocents still in the game.


## Number of Culprits

| Players | Culprits |
|---------|----------|
| 4       | 1        |
| 5 - 6   | 2        |
| 7 - 9   | 3        |
| 10      | 4        |


## Tips

- Culprits: stay calm, blend in, and point fingers at others.
- Innocents: pay attention to what people say and use the clues wisely.
- Watch for anyone who changes their story or acts nervous.


## Setup

1. Open the game in your browser.
2. Enter a crew name.
3. Select the number of players.
4. Enter each player's name.
5. Each player taps their card privately to see their role.
6. The game begins.


## Notes

- The game runs fully in the browser. No download needed.
- Each game picks a random scenario, so no two games are the same.
- Best played in person, passing the device around for role reveals.
